bpy.ops.view2d.ndof()¶

Use a 3D mouse device to pan/zoom the view

Pan the view

Parameters:
  • deltax (int in [-inf, inf], (optional)) – Delta X
  • deltay (int in [-inf, inf], (optional)) – Delta Y
bpy.ops.view2d.reset()¶

Reset the view

bpy.ops.view2d.scroll_down(deltax=0, deltay=0, page=False)¶

Parameters:

Parameters:
