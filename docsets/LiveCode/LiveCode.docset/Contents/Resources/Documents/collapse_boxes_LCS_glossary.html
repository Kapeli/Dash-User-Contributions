collapse boxes

Typeglossary
DictionaryLCS
Synonymswindowshade,windowshade box,collapse box,collapse boxes,collapse
EditionCommunity
RelatedGlossary: title bar, Mac OS
Description

Box at the right end of a window's title bar (on Mac OSsystems) that hides everything but the title bar when you click it. Clicking the collapse box again reveals the window contents.
