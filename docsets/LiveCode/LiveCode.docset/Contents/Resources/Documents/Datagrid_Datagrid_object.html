Datagrid
Summary

An object for forms or tables

Description

The Data Grid enables you to integrate powerful tables and forms into your LiveCode projects. Data grids combine LiveCode groups and behaviors to provide you with a simple, yet flexible means of displaying your data in just about any way you want. See http://lessons.livecode.com/m/datagrid/l/7301-what-is-the-data-grid for full documentation, lessons, and tutorials.
