Synonymscolor specifier,color reference,color descriptor
EditionCommunity
RelatedGlossary: HTML
Description

Any valid way of describing a particular color that LiveCode can understand.

Colors can be referred to by color name, HTML -style specification, or as three comma-separated numbers between zero and 255 (one for each of red, blue, and green).
