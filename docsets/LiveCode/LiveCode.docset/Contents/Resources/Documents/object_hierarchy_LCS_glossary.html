RelatedGlossary: card, stack, object, grouped control, substack, owner, card control, group, main stack
Description

The chain of ownership in which one object is owned by another. A card control is owned by the card it's on; a grouped control are owned by its group; a card is owned by the stack it's in; and substacks are owned by their main stack.

Also, the chain of all the objects above a specific object in the hierarchy.
