straight quote

Typeglossary
DictionaryLCS
Synonymsdouble quote,straight quote,quote mark,quotation mark
EditionCommunity
RelatedGlossary: statement, character, LiveCode
Description

The double straight quote character, ASCII 34 (").

Double quotes are not interchangeable with "smart quotes" or curved quotes in LiveCode statements. A double quote is also not the same as two single quote characters ('').
