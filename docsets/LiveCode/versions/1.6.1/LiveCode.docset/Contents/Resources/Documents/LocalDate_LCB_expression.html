RelatedExpression: UniversalDate
Description

Returns the local date as a list of seven numeric components. The elements of the list are:

  • The year
  • The month (1-12)
  • The day of the month (1-31, depending on the month)
  • The hour (0-23)
  • The minute (0-59)
  • The second (0-59)
  • The offset from UTC in seconds
