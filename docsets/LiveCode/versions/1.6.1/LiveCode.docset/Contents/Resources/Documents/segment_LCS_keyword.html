Description

Use the segment keyword to refer to one or more words in a container.

A segment is delimited by one or more spaces, tabs, or returns, or enclosed by double quotes. A single segment can contain multiple char|characters and multiple items, but not multiple lines.

Note: Segments are delimited by double quotes ("), but not by curved quotes (Ò or Ó). LiveCode does not treat curved quotes as quotes.
